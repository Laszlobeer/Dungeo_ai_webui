How to Play

Welcome to the RPG Adventure! Here's how to get started:

  1. Select an AI model to power your adventure
  2. Create your character by choosing a genre and role
  3. Enter the game and start making choices

Game Mechanics

Every choice you make has consequences that permanently alter the game world.

Controls

  • /censored - Toggle content filtering
  • /redo - Regenerate the last response
  • /save - Save your adventure
  • /consequences - View recent consequences
